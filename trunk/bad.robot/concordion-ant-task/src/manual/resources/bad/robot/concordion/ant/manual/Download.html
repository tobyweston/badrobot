Maven Central

Configure your pom.xml using the information below.

First, configure the repository; 

<repositories>
    <repository>
        <id>bad.robot</id>
        <name>bad.robot repository on robotooling</name>
        <url>http://www.robotooling.com/maven/</url>
    </repository>
</repositories>

Add a dependency; 

<dependency>
    <groupId>bad.robot</groupId>
    <artifactId>concordion-ant-task</artifactId>
    <version>1.1</version>
</dependency>

Direct

You can also download the jar directly from 

http://www.robotooling.com/maven/bad/robot/concordion-ant-task/

or from the terminal 

$ curl http://www.robotooling.com/maven/bad/robot1.1/concordion-ant-task-1.1.jar > concordion-ant-task-1.1.jar
$ mvn install:install-file -Dfile=concordion-ant-task-1.1.jar -DgroupId=bad.robot -DartifactId=concordion-ant-task -Dversion=1.1 -Dpackaging=jar
